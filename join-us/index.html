Join Us

The Frederick Douglass Bicentennial thrives through community participation. There are many ways to get involved.

Ways to Participate

  • Attend Meet-Ups: Join our regular discussions and events
  • Volunteer: Help organize events, create educational materials, or assist with outreach
  • Spread the Word: Share our mission with your network
  • Educate: Use our resources in your classroom or community group
  • Donate: Support our programs and events

Newsletter

Subscribe to our newsletter to stay informed about upcoming events, new resources, and opportunities to engage with Douglass's legacy.
