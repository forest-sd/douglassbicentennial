The Meet-Ups

Our meet-ups bring together people who share an interest in Frederick Douglass's life, writings, and ongoing relevance to contemporary issues of justice and equality.

What to Expect

  • Readings from Douglass's speeches and autobiographies
  • Facilitated discussions connecting historical themes to current events
  • Guest speakers including historians, activists, and educators
  • Community-building in a welcoming, inclusive environment

Past Themes

  • Douglass and the Power of Literacy
  • The Fourth of July Speech: Then and Now
  • Douglass on Women's Rights
  • Photography and Self-Representation in Douglass's Era

Check back for upcoming meet-up dates and locations, or contact us to be added to our mailing list.
